Our Journey

MIT Computer Training Center began in 1999 as a small initiative to bring IT education to Bopitiya. At a time when ICT access was limited, we became one of the first pioneers to introduce structured computer education in the area.

In 2001, the institute was officially registered as a sole proprietorship business, marking the beginning of professional training.

In 2014, we were registered under TVEC, and in 2020, we achieved accreditation to conduct NVQ courses.

Today, MIT stands as a leading ICT training center in the region, empowering students with practical skills and real-world knowledge.
